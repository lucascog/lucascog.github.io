What is Lucky Miner?

– It’s a simple game that gives you a chance to win money in a weekly drawing. Behind the scenes, it is using a very small amount of your computer’s unused capacity to mine cryptocurrency.


Cryptocurrency? That’s kind of scary, isn’t it?

– It shouldn’t be. We want to help the world understand how cryptocurrency works. Lucky Miner allows everyone to participate and learn without making risky investments.

What is mining?

– Cryptocurrency mining is a process of solving math problems to unlock value. By contributing your unused computing capacity, you’re adding value to the Lucky Miner community pool and increasing your chance to win the weekly drawing.


Are you using my CPU?

- Yes. We use a small amount of your computing power which is carefully managed to not interfere with your daily tasks.


Are you using my personal data?

- Never. The only store your email address and have no other reason.


What about electricity and battery life?

- Lucky Miner uses a very small amount of your computer’s power, so you shouldn’t notice any major difference. But if you’d like to conserve battery power, you can pause Lucky Miner in the app at any time.
